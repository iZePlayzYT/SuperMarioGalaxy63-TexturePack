Super Mario Galaxy 63 – 4K Texture Pack (DDS)

This 4K texture pack is for Super Mario Galaxy 63, a ROM hack of Super Mario Galaxy 2.
It improves the game's visuals with higher quality textures optimized for Dolphin Emulator.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
INSTALLATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Install the latest version of Dolphin Emulator:
   https://dolphin-emu.org/

2. Enable custom textures:
   - In Dolphin, go to Graphics Settings → Advanced
   - Enable: Load Custom Textures
   - Disable: Prefetch Custom Textures

3. From this ZIP, copy the included "SB4" folder into your Load/Textures directory:
   Windows: C:\Users\<YourUsername>\AppData\Roaming\Dolphin Emulator\Load\Textures\
   macOS:   ~/Library/Application Support/Dolphin/Load/Textures/
   Linux:   ~/.local/share/dolphin-emu/Load/Textures/

Important:
- The "SB4" folder works for all game versions.
- If you use the SB4 folder from the ZIP, make sure there are no version-specific folders
  (SB4E01, SB4P01, SB4J01, etc.) in Load/Textures/, or some textures from SB4 may be ignored.
